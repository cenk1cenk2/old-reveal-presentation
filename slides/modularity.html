Modularity

  • Expand the selection of supported devices easily.
  • Use the same database backbone and input/output interfaces for every device.
  • No tinkering on frontend side.

Modularity is the most important aspect for me for further increasing the supported device types in the future.

It will share the same database backbone and depending on the type of device input/output interfaces will be same therefore no tinkering will be needed on frontend side.
