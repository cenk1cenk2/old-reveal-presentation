What can be a viable workflow these days to develop a web application?

While the development techniques for web has varied over time, this is also true for suplementary workflow that enables this application to be a product or have an end result. Although I insist on there are no rules of thumbs in web development, these suplementary actions proves it otherwise albeit it is considered to be in a different area.

What will be the future of web development given that there are two distinct architectures of monolithic and microservices?

With the urge of working with many people in one single project and to reach the whole world by the means of scaling the services, it has been a good practice to split a large application in to smaller parts by technology giants. But the case is this trend is catching on with smaller companies as well as individuals. So I think this is the most important question that needs to be answered for foreseeing the future of the web development.
